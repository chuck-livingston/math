Projects & Resources

  • KnotInfo & LinkInfo
    Tables of knot and link invariants, maintained with Allison Moore.
  • Knot Concordance Calculator
    Expresses any linear combination of low crossing number knots in terms of an independent set, and computes the algebraic and geometric order in the concordance group. Built by Julia Collins.
  • Fox–Milnor (1957)
    Fox and Milnor’s announcement concerning the knot concordance group. Bull. AMS, 1957, p. 402.
  • Knot Theory: Carus Monograph
    Information concerning my book on knot theory in the MAA Carus Monograph Series.
